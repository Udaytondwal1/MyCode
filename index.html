Positive Health Zone is a holistic healthcare organization established under the leadership of Dr. Anil K. Gupta. Our approach to holistic health care focuses on the body, mind, and spirit to achieve optimal health by addressing various dimensions of health: physical, emotional, intellectual, social, spiritual, environmental, and occupational. Lifestyle modification and stress management are crucial to tackling most health issues.

Positive health predicts increased longevity, decreased health costs, better mental health in aging, and improved prognosis when illness strikes. PHZ's aim is to encourage the prevention of disease and the regeneration of health and well-being. Our passion is to empower people to take responsibility for their health and well-being by promoting the connection between mind, body, brain, and soul. We recognize the need for physical activity, healthy foods, adequate sleep, and avoiding risky behaviors involving unsafe activities and substances.
